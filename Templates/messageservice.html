Messages Service

Messaging - User Guide

Note: The user must be logged in.

How to access the messaging service?

Simply click on My Messages, in the Personal Space to see the different exchanges and discussions.

For more precision, messages will be classified into 2 groups:

  • Messaging as a giver.
  • Messaging as a receiver.
How to contact a user?

If the user finds a donation that interests them, they can request it by clicking on the "Contact" button.

Once the first message is sent, the conversation can be found in the My Messages tab of the user's account.

Does the giver have to respond to all messages from requesters?

As some donations attract the attention of many users, givers are free to choose their beneficiaries and will be contacted by message if they wish to follow up.

What happens if a user is in contact with the giver and the donation is already taken?

The discussion between the giver and the beneficiary will be automatically closed.
